## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Relying 100% on AI Output
AI is a tool, not a replacement. Always add your expertise and editing.

### 2. Undercharging
Don't charge $5 for work that takes hours. Value your time + AI advantage.

### 3. No Specialization
"AI services" is too broad. Pick a niche: "AI automation for dental practices"

### 4. Ignoring Quality
Fast ≠ good enough. Use AI for speed, but maintain quality standards.
